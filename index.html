    ADVANTAGES

    For your construction projects

    With the Benetics app, you and your team can do more in less time: Hello digitalization!

    Current plans for everyone

    Old plans lead to construction errors. With Benetics, that's history. Upload a new plan via PC and every employee has it on their mobile phone. The plans can even be used with Zoom down to the smallest detail without mobile phone reception. Always and everywhere.
    Learn more

    To-do lists instead of dead times

    Unnecessary sitting around on the construction site? Given. With Benetics, all employees know what to do and where. Because all open tasks can be seen on the plans. So everyone can always do the work. Even if the foreman or project manager is not on site or available.
    Learn more

    Director's reports via voice assistant

    Directing work is worth hard cash. Stupid if they are not reported. The Benetics Language Assistant allows anyone to report on the work they have done - in completely natural language, even in dialect and foreign languages. And on the PC there is a nice overview of all reports, which can then be calculated in a flash.
    Learn more
